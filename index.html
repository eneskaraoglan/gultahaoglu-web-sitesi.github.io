About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering, Karadeniz Technical University (KTU). My research interests lie at the intersection of Multimedia Security, Computer Vision, and Artificial Intelligence.

I am particularly passionate about digital forensics, focusing on detecting manipulated content such as Deepfakes. My work aims to develop robust AI techniques to ensure the integrity of digital media in an era of disinformation.
